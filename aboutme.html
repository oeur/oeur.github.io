About Me

I am a friendly graduate student in the Department of Physics at UC Merced working with Dr. Sarah Loebman on using simulations to research the evolution of stars in the galactic disk.



Research Interests: Particularly, I am interested in the average speed of stars that are located within the same vicinity, that have the same radius or distance from the galactic center. Using high-resolution simulations, I am drawing comparisons of stellar behavior to SDSS observations. Ultimately, I want to understand the mechanism that causes groups of stars to co-orbit.



Hobbies: Hiking, traveling, & getting to know new people! :)
